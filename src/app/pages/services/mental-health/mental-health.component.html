Mental Health

Our specialist mental health services
We work collaboratively with Health and Specialist mental health services to provide assistance to those who have a diagnosed mental illness, which can range from:
  • Panic and anxiety disorders
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Depression
  • Psychological problems
The people we serve build self-confidence and increased emotional wellbeing on their path to independence and recovery via self-development and collaborative support with our team.
